About RC

  • I'm a non-binary QPOC who has a passion for visually stimulating, lightweight front-end technologies. I have 1 year of experience teaching prospective engineering students at Learners Guild, Black Girls Code and helped to grow Lil Bytes, a coding program, to help teach children of all ages in the multi-faceted and complex elements of Javascript, HTML, CSS and other frameworks/libraries.

    I fell in love with coding and creating simple, complex applications with a few keystrokes a long time ago and consider myself a natural teacher that enjoys sharing my knowledge with others. I thrive off of drawing from my own personal experience and explaining engineering concepts in a way that anyone, including those with little to no prior technical knowledge, can understand.
